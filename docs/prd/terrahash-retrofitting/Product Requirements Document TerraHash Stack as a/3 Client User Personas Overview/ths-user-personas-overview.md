## **The Five Core Personas:**

### **1. Regional Mining Operator ("The Independent")**

- Scale: 2-10 MW
- Decision-Makers: Owner + finance partner (8-12 week cycle)
- Pain Points: Summer throttling, labor intensity, aging hardware
- Value Driver: ROI/payback period
- SLA Preference: Gold (semi-annual support)
- Revenue Potential: $1-3M LTV

### **2. Institutional Mining Company ("The Scalable Operator")**

- Scale: 50-200 MW across multiple sites
- Decision-Makers: CTO/VP Operations + CFO + Board (12-16 weeks)
- Pain Points: Quarterly efficiency pressure, infrastructure heterogeneity, talent retention
- Value Driver: Fleet standardization & institutional compliance
- SLA Preference: Platinum (dedicated account team, 99.5% uptime)
- Revenue Potential: $5-15M LTV

### **3. Energy Producer / Co-Location Provider ("The Infrastructure Player")**

- Scale: 10-50 MW (secondary business line)
- Decision-Makers: VP Operations + VP Finance + Board (16-24 weeks)
- Pain Points: Expertise gap, operational handoff, asset stranding
- Value Driver: Turnkey solution, minimal internal effort
- SLA Preference: Platinum with white-glove integration
- Revenue Potential: $2-8M LTV

### **4. Distressed Asset Acquirer ("The Value Investor")**

- Scale: 20-100 MW rapid acquisitions
- Decision-Makers: PE sponsor investment committee (4-8 weeks COMPRESSED)
- Pain Points: Rapid assessment, execution risk, financing sensitivity
- Value Driver: Fixed-price retrofit, performance guarantees
- SLA Preference: Platinum (intensive first 90 days)
- Revenue Potential: $3-6M LTV

### **5. Institutional Investment Fund / Public Miner ("The Capital-Backed Player")**

- Scale: 200+ MW global operations
- Decision-Makers: Chief Mining Officer + CFO + Board (8-16 weeks)
- Pain Points: Competitive differentiation, treasury complexity, ESG compliance
- Value Driver: Fleet standardization, institutional credibility
- SLA Preference: Platinum enterprise (board-ready reporting)
- Revenue Potential: $10-30M+ LTV
