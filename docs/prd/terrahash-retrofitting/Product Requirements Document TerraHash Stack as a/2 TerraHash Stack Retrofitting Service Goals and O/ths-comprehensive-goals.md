- **Develop standardized retrofit designs supporting 5-6 primary facility archetypes:**

    2. **Warehouse hot/cold aisle: 3-20 MW** - Zone-by-zone conversion, phased deployment
    3. **Existing immersion: 2-15 MW** - Standardization and optimization of legacy systems
    4. **Hybrid (mixed cooling): 5-25 MW** - High-density zones (liquid) + legacy zones (optimized air)
    5. **Data hall/colocation: 10-50 MW** - Centralized cooling plant architecture
    6. **Legacy/greenfield: 1-50 MW** - Custom design framework for unique sites
