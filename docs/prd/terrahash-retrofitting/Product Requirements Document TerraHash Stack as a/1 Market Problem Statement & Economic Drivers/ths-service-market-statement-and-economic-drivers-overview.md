## **PART 1: MARKET PAIN POINTS - AIR-COOLED FACILITIES**

**1.1 Energy Inefficiency & Excessive Operating Costs:**

**Pain Point: Air Cooling Consumes 25-40% More Energy**

- Air thermal conductivity 25x lower than water/coolants
- ASIC fans consume 200-400W per miner just for cooling
- Facility HVAC requires additional 20-30% of total power
- Every 10Â°C temperature increase = 3-5% higher power consumption

**Quantified Impact:**

| **Metric** | **Air-Cooled** | **Liquid-Cooled** | **Improvement** |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| ASIC Power (per TH) | 22-26 J/TH | 15-18 J/TH | 25-35% |
| Cooling Infrastructure | 25-30% of ASIC | 3-5% of ASIC | 85-90% |
| Total Power | 28-34 J/TH | 15.5-19 J/TH | 40-45% |
| Annual Cost (100 TH, $0.06/kWh) | $14,700 | $8,200 | **$6,500 savings** |

**10 MW Facility Impact:**

- Air-cooled: $3.5-4.5M annual electricity
- Liquid-cooled: $2.2-2.8M annual electricity
- **Annual Savings: $1.2-1.7M (30-40% reduction)**

**Thermal Throttling Revenue Loss:**

- 5-15% hashrate reduction during peak heat = 5-15% revenue loss
- 10 MW facility: $200-600K annual lost revenue from throttling

**Equipment Wear & Premature Failure:**

- Dust/contamination reduces efficiency 10-20%
- ASIC lifespan: 2-3 years (air) vs. 6-8 years (liquid)
- Replacement cost savings: $800-1,050 per ASIC annually

**1.2 Operational Complexity & Labor Intensity:**

**Environmental & Noise Pollution:**

- 75-90 dB noise (equivalent to lawn mower)

**1.3 Scalability & Density Limitations:**

**Low Power Density Constraints:**
